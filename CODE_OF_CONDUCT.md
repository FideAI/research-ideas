# Code of Conduct

Fide AI research discussions should be rigorous, charitable, and evidence-driven.

Contributors should:

- engage ideas directly;
- distinguish evidence from speculation;
- respect religious differences and reviewer expertise;
- avoid personal attacks;
- avoid publishing private or sensitive information;
- follow Fide AI claims boundaries.

Maintainers may close issues or pull requests that are abusive, promotional,
off-topic, unsafe, or inconsistent with the repository's public-interest purpose.
