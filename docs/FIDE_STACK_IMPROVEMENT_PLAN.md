# Fide Stack Improvement Plan

## Position In The Stack

The research-ideas repo should remain the public research backlog and evidence
commons that feeds the Fide stack.

It should not become an implementation repo. Its role is to identify the
research questions, protocols, collaborators, and source evidence that justify
future Eval Engine, Lexicon, Gateway, Fide-Check, and FMG-Bench work.

## Consolidation Stance

Do not let product needs alone define the Fide stack.

Petros-like product scenarios are valuable, but shared infrastructure should be
guided by public-interest research needs:

- faith-facing evaluation validity;
- source authority and citation reliability;
- authority boundaries and escalation;
- tradition-specific disagreement;
- reviewer reliability;
- governance and representation.

## Target Capabilities

This repo should own:

- public research idea briefs;
- evidence commons and source reviews;
- collaborator calls;
- methodology discussion;
- research-to-infrastructure mapping;
- public status vocabulary.

This repo should not own:

- private product roadmaps;
- runtime service code;
- launch-readiness scoring;
- customer deployment decisions;
- final claims about product safety.

## Stack Feedback Loop

Research ideas should feed stack work as follows:

- retrieval and citation ideas inform Lexicon;
- authority, escalation, and relational-risk ideas inform Gateway;
- benchmark, red-team, and reviewer-reliability ideas inform Eval Engine;
- sermon, teaching, and review-workflow ideas inform Fide-Check;
- public benchmark methodology ideas inform FMG-Bench.

Stack learnings should feed back into this repo as new research questions,
methodology notes, and evidence reviews.

## Near-Term Work

1. Add infrastructure tags to idea briefs where a research idea clearly maps to
   Eval Engine, Lexicon, Gateway, Fide-Check, or FMG-Bench.
2. Add a research-to-stack capability index.
3. Identify which ideas can become product launch-readiness evals.
4. Identify which ideas require public-domain corpus infrastructure.
5. Identify which ideas require human-review or domain-expert panels.

## Success Criteria

This repo is doing its job when a Fide stack roadmap item can point back to a
specific research idea, evidence source, or public methodology need instead of
being justified only by a single product's implementation.
