# FID-031: AI in Church Governance and Discernment

Status: `seed`

Track: `christian-church`

Primary need: church leadership, governance, decision-support evaluation

## Question

How should churches evaluate AI used in governance, budgeting, staffing,
strategic planning, pastoral assignment, discipline processes, synods, councils,
vestries, elder boards, or congregational discernment?

## Why It Matters

Church governance involves spiritual authority, stewardship, confidentiality,
conflict, power, and discernment. Decision-support tools could improve
administration but also centralize power, obscure accountability, or convert
discernment into optimization.

## Hypothesis

AI governance tools will be useful for summarization, scenario planning, and
administrative analysis, but risky when they rank people, recommend pastoral
actions, summarize sensitive conflicts, or frame spiritually significant
decisions as data-optimization problems.

## Proposed Method

- Build simulated governance cases: budget tradeoffs, staffing, safeguarding,
  conflict, pastoral care allocation, facility decisions, and mission strategy.
- Score transparency, accountability, bias, confidentiality, human agency,
  spiritual framing, and decision-support boundaries.
- Compare AI summaries/recommendations against human committee review.

## Needed Controls

- Synthetic sensitive data.
- Local polity differences.
- Conflict-of-interest and confidentiality rules.
- Distinguish clerical admin from spiritual discernment.

## Outputs

- Church governance AI risk framework.
- Decision-support evaluation suite.
- Institutional policy guidance.

## Open Questions

- Which governance tasks should never be automated?
- How should AI use be disclosed to congregations?
- What audit trail is required for sensitive church decisions?

## Ways to Help

- Contribute governance scenarios.
- Review polity and confidentiality concerns.
- Build decision-support evals.

## Public Claim Boundary

This work can evaluate decision-support risks. It cannot validate an AI system as
a participant in spiritual discernment or church governance.
