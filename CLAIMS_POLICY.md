# Claims Policy

This repository is a public research idea commons. It contains hypotheses,
project briefs, collaboration requests, and methodology discussions.

Do not cite an idea brief as a Fide AI finding.

Fide AI findings require a separate public artifact such as:

- a technical report;
- a benchmark release;
- a paper;
- a public field brief;
- a leaderboard snapshot with methodology and limitations.

## Allowed Claims

It is acceptable to say:

- "Fide AI is scoping this research idea."
- "This idea is open for collaboration."
- "This brief proposes a possible evaluation method."

## Disallowed Claims

Do not say:

- "Fide AI has validated this result."
- "This model is safe for faith-facing use."
- "This benchmark proves pastoral competence."
- "This idea represents official doctrine or institutional endorsement."

unless a separate reviewed Fide AI publication explicitly supports that claim.
